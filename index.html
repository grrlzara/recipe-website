Pork Noodle Stir-Fry

This quick and easy stir-fry recipe is the perfect mid-week meal. It takes 30 minutes to make and is great for busy evenings.

Recipe Information:

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Number of servings: 4

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 350g lean pork mince
  • 350g egg noodles
  • thumb-sized piece of ginger
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 320g stir-fry vegetable mix
  • 4 tbsp low-salt soy sauce
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 4 tbsp sweet chilli sauce

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Heat the oil in a wok or frying pan.
  2. Step 2: Add the mince, break it up with a spoon and fry over a high heat for about 8 mins until browning.
  3. Step 3: While the meat cooks, boil a kettle, then pour the hot water over the noodles. Set aside for 5-10 mins to soften.
  4. Step 4: Add the ginger, garlic and veg to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 mins.
  5. Step 5: Mix 1 tbsp soy sauce with the cornflour to make a paste. Add the remaining soy sauce, the chilli sauce and 2 tbsp water.
  6. Step 6: Drain the noodles and add to the pan with the sauce.
  7. Step 7: Cook until the sauce coats the noodles, adding a splash of water if needed, then serve.

Additional Notes:

For extra flavor, add red pepper flakes or top with grated parmesan cheese.
